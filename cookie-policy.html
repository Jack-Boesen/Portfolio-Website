What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used to enable website functionality or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.

How I Use Cookies

I only use cookies for basic analytical purposes through Google Analytics. This service helps me understand how visitors interact with the website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

Google Analytics cookies may collect:

  • Your IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and settings

  • General geographic location (not precise)

This information helps me improve the functionality and content of the site.
