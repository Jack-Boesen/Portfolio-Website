This is my personal website, and I respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what kind of information is collected when you visit, and how I use that information.

What I Collect

I don’t collect any personal information from you directly. However, I do use Google Analytics to understand how people interact with my site. Google Analytics collects anonymous data such as:

  • Which pages you visit
  • How long you stay on the site
  • What browser and device you’re using

  • Your IP address (anonymized by Google)

Why I Use This Information

I use this data to improve my website—like seeing which pages are most visited or whether people are accessing it on mobile or desktop. It helps me make the site better and more useful for visitors like you.
